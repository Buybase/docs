#### General Shopping Assistant
Create a sophisticated AI shopping companion using Buybase that revolutionizes the online shopping experience. This assistant can:
- Search and analyze products across multiple e-commerce platforms
- Compare prices and find the best deals
- Track order status and delivery updates
- Learn user preferences over time

#### Brand-Specific Shopping Assistant
Enhance your brand's digital presence by integrating Buybase into your existing website or app. This specialized assistant can:
- Provide personalized product recommendations
- Help customers discover your full product catalog
- Automate reordering of frequently purchased items
- Offer real-time customer support for shopping-related queries

#### General AI Agent with Shopping Capabilities
Supercharge your AI agent with Buybase's powerful e-commerce capabilities:
- Seamlessly handle shopping-related tasks within broader conversations
- Manage shopping lists and wishlists
